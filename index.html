LEARN HOW TO SAVE

A variable interest rate is subject to change over time since it is influenced by the repo rate (set by the South African Reserve Bank). Taking a savings account with a variable interest rate means you earn more interest when the country interest rates go up and earn less when the rate is low. This means, interest being earned on savings accounts is currently low; also interest being paid on loans is low.
